Our Story

Why VBuilt Exists

We noticed a massive gap in India's home services market: finding reliable, verified, and transparent skilled professionals was exhausting.

Building Trust from Zero 🚀

VBuilt was born from a simple idea: "We Build & Secure". We realized that whether it's fixing a leaking pipe, wiring a new home, or installing complex CCTV systems, customers needed someone they could trust with their homes and businesses.

We are a tech-driven marketplace on a mission to organize the unorganized sector of construction and maintenance. By bringing together the finest tradespeople—electricians, plumbers, masons, and architects—we provide a seamless, transparent, and high-quality service experience.
